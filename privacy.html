Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026 · Verava, Pune, India

1. Who We Are

Verava ("we", "our", "us") is an AI voice agent platform operated by Niinaad Patil, based in Pune, Maharashtra, India. We provide AI-powered voice agent services to Indian businesses under the brand name Verava. Our website is verava.in and our dashboard is accessible at app.verava.in.

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at hello@verava.in.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Business client information: Name, email address, phone number, business name, and payment details when you sign up for Verava.
  • Call data: Phone numbers of callers, call duration, call recordings, transcripts, and extracted variables (name, service requested, etc.) from calls handled by Vera.
  • Dashboard usage data: Login activity, feature usage, and session information.
  • Communication data: Emails and messages you send us for support or onboarding.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To set up and operate your Vera AI voice agent
  • To provide you access to the Verava dashboard
  • To send you call logs, summaries, and performance reports
  • To process billing and payments
  • To improve Vera's performance and accuracy
  • To respond to your support requests
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Call Recordings and Transcripts

Vera records and transcribes calls handled on your business number. These recordings and transcripts are accessible to you (the business client) and to Verava for service operation and quality purposes. End callers are not separately notified by Verava — it is the responsibility of the business client to comply with applicable laws regarding call recording consent in their jurisdiction.

Call recordings are stored securely and are not shared with third parties outside of our infrastructure providers.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with advertisers. We may share data with:

  • Infrastructure providers: Voice AI and cloud infrastructure used to operate Vera (under confidentiality agreements)
  • Payment processors: For processing subscription payments (Razorpay)
  • Legal authorities: If required by law or court order

6. Data Retention

We retain your call data and account information for as long as your account is active. If you cancel your account, we will retain data for 90 days before deletion, unless you request earlier deletion. You can request deletion of your data at any time by emailing hello@verava.in.

7. Security

We use industry-standard encryption and access controls to protect your data. Access to call data is restricted to authorised Verava personnel and to the business client via the dashboard. We do not store payment card details — all payments are processed via Razorpay's secure infrastructure.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing (subject to contractual obligations)

To exercise these rights, email hello@verava.in.

9. Cookies

Our website and dashboard use minimal cookies for authentication and session management only. We do not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify active clients of significant changes via email. Continued use of Verava after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
