Add-On Features

Supercharge Vera

Start with what you need. Add more as your business grows. Every add-on activates within 24 hours.

📊
Smart Analytics
₹999 / month
Top 10 caller topics, peak call hours, call intent breakdown (inquiry vs complaint vs booking), week-over-week trends. Includes a branded Weekly Intelligence Report every Monday.
Best for: Owners who want to make smarter decisions based on what customers actually ask — not guesswork.
🎯
Lead Scoring
₹1 per call · Pro plan only
After every call, Vera tags the caller as Hot, Warm, or Cold based on the conversation. Your team sees this in the dashboard — so they always know who to follow up with first.
Best for: Real estate agencies and coaching institutes where follow-up speed decides whether you win the deal.
💬
WhatsApp Follow-up
₹1,499 / month
The moment a call ends, Vera automatically sends a WhatsApp message — appointment confirmation, price list, directions, brochure, whatever you preset. Zero manual effort from your staff.
Best for: Clinics confirming appointments, institutes sending details, agencies sharing property info.
📅
Weekly Intelligence Report
Included with Smart Analytics
Every Monday, a clean summary — total calls, top topics, lead quality, one key recommendation. Glance at it in 60 seconds and know exactly how your business performed.
Best for: Busy owners who want insight without logging in every day.
