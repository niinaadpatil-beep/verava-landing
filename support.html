Support

How can we help you?

Find answers to common questions about Vera, your plan, billing, and setup.

General
What is Verava and what does Vera do? +

Verava is an AI voice agent platform built for Indian businesses. Vera is your AI Business Representative — she answers every inbound call 24/7, handles appointment bookings, answers FAQs, qualifies leads, and gives your callers a professional experience without any human staff involved. She speaks Hindi, English, and Hinglish naturally.

Is Vera a robot or a real person? +

Vera is an AI — but she speaks naturally and is trained specifically for your business, your tone, and your services. Most callers don't ask. If a caller directly asks whether they're speaking to an AI, Vera is designed to be transparent. We never deceive.

Which industries does Verava work for? +

Healthcare (clinics, diagnostic centers, hospitals), Education (coaching institutes, schools, colleges), Real Estate (builders, brokers, developers), Showrooms (auto, jewellery, retail), and professional services (CA firms, law firms, consultancies). If your business gets phone calls, Vera can handle them.

What languages does Vera speak? +

Vera speaks English, Hindi, and Hinglish — and adapts to however the caller speaks. If a caller starts in Hindi, Vera responds in Hindi. Regional language support (Marathi, etc.) is available on request.

Setup & Onboarding
How long does setup take? +

Typically 3–5 business days from the time we receive your business details. We build your custom Vera — name, voice, script, FAQs — and test it before going live. You review and approve before Vera takes a single call.

What information do you need from me to set up Vera? +

Your business name, services you offer, pricing (if you want Vera to share it), working hours, location, how you want appointments booked, and 10–15 common questions your callers ask. We guide you through this during onboarding — it takes about 30 minutes.

Can I update Vera's script or add new services later? +

Yes. You can request script updates, new FAQs, pricing changes, or service additions at any time. Minor updates are handled within 24–48 hours. Major re-training takes 2–3 business days. Contact us at support@verava.in.

Do I need any technical knowledge to use Verava? +

No. You don't need to write code, configure anything technical, or manage any software. We handle the entire setup. You give us your business details, we build Vera, and you start receiving handled calls. Your only interface is the Verava dashboard — which is as simple as reading a table.

Billing & Plans
What does the ₹3,999 setup fee cover? +

It covers everything in your custom Vera build — persona creation, voice configuration, script writing, FAQ training, and full onboarding support. It's a one-time fee. You pay it once, never again, regardless of how long you stay on the platform.

What happens if I use more minutes than my plan includes? +

No calls get dropped. Vera keeps answering. Extra minutes are billed at your plan's per-minute rate — ₹9/min on Starter, ₹8.50/min on Growth, ₹8/min on Pro. You'll see usage in real time on your dashboard.

Do unused minutes roll over? +

Yes. Unused minutes from your monthly plan roll over to the next month. They don't expire.

Is there a contract or minimum commitment? +

No long-term contracts. All plans are month-to-month. The setup fee is one-time regardless of tenure. We're confident enough in the product that we don't need to lock you in.

How do I upgrade or downgrade my plan? +

Contact us at billing@verava.in or WhatsApp Ninaad directly. Plan changes take effect from the next billing cycle. There's no penalty for changing plans.

About Vera
Will my customers know they're talking to an AI? +

Vera speaks naturally and is trained to handle conversations fluently. Most callers don't ask. If a caller directly asks, Vera is transparent. We never deceive — but we do deliver a genuinely good experience that reflects well on your business.

Can Vera transfer a call to a human if needed? +

Yes. Vera can be configured to transfer calls to a specific number when a caller requests a human, or when a conversation reaches a point that requires human judgment. This is set up during onboarding based on your preference.

Does Vera work on weekends and holidays? +

Yes. Vera is available 24/7/365 — weekends, public holidays, nights. She never takes a day off. This is one of the core reasons businesses use Verava — eliminating the gap between when customers call and when staff is available.

What can I see in the dashboard? +

Every call your number receives — caller name, number, what they asked about, call duration, full transcript, AI summary, sentiment score, call recording (if enabled), and outcome (appointment booked, callback scheduled, etc.). Everything is visible in real time.

Technical
Do I need to change my existing phone number? +

No. We work with your existing business number. The setup process involves configuring call forwarding so Vera picks up when your line is busy or unanswered — or as your primary handler. We walk you through this during onboarding.

Where is my call data stored? Is it secure? +

Call data is stored securely on encrypted servers. We don't share your data or your clients' data with any third parties. You can view and delete call records from your dashboard at any time. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

What happens if Vera goes down or there's an outage? +

We maintain high uptime SLAs. In the rare event of an issue, your calls will either fall through to your existing number or receive a configured fallback message. Growth and Pro plan clients receive dedicated support with faster response times.

Can I integrate Vera with my existing CRM or calendar? +

Yes. Vera supports Google Calendar for appointment booking. CRM integrations (Google Sheets, Airtable, HubSpot, etc.) are available. Contact us at support@verava.in to discuss your specific integration needs.
